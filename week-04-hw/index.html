Ultimate Chocolate Chip Cookies

by C.C. Baker

The Ultimate Cookie Book by C.C. Baker has sold over 1,000 copies.

Best chocolate chip cookies I have ever had! Truely the ULTIMATE chocolate chip cookies. -Rachel Jay

Total: 1 hr 5 min
Yield: 30 Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 (12-ounce) bag semisweet chocolate chips, or chunks
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon fine salt

Directions

  1. Evenly position 2 racks in the middle of the oven and preheat to 375 degrees F.
    • on convection setting if you have it. Cookies will be terrible without convection!
  2. Put the butter in a microwave safe bowl, cover and microwave on medium power until melted. Cool slightly.
    • Alternatively melt in a small saucepan.
  3. Whisk the sugars, eggs, butter and vanilla in a large bowl until smooth.
  4. Whisk the flour, baking soda and salt in another bowl.
  5. Stir the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients; Stir in the chocolate chips or chunks.
    • take care not to over mix.
  6. Scoop heaping tablespoons of the dough onto the prepared pans. Space the cookies about 2-inches apart on the pans.
  7. Bake, until golden, but still soft in the center, 12 to 16 minutes, depending on how chewy or crunchy you like your cookies.
  8. Transfer hot cookies with a spatula to a rack to cool. Serve.

Store cookies in a tightly sealed container for up to 5 8 days.
